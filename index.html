YT—mono

  1. Paste a YouTube video ID

  2. Buffering...0%

  3. Assign notes to times (in seconds)

  4. Play! via MIDI or your computer keyboard

  5. Share your work

 - Please don't do anything commercial with the art. I feel funny about people selling art created in minutes through a tool that took days to build.

If you use YT—mono as input to your creative process, and the output is so different that it would be unrecognizeable as a YT—mono design, please feel free to use it commercially, or however you wish. The art is 100% yours at that point.
